About Me

I am Joshua Nakpri, a dedicated politician, innovative entrepreneur, and passionate farmer committed to driving positive change in our communities. With a deep-rooted belief in service, I have dedicated my political career to advocating for policies that promote economic growth, agricultural development, and sustainable business practices. As a farmer, I understand the challenges faced by hardworking individuals in the agricultural sector. I actively work towards implementing solutions that enhance food security, support rural development, and modernize farming techniques to ensure a prosperous future for the next generation. Beyond politics and farming, I am a thriving entrepreneur who believes in the power of business to transform lives. Through my ventures, I have created jobs, empowered young entrepreneurs, and championed innovation to foster economic self-sufficiency. My mission is to build a society where businesses flourish, farmers thrive, and governance is transparent and people-centered. With a strong commitment to integrity and progress, I continue to work towards a brighter and more prosperous future for all.
